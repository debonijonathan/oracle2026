Behind the model

How It Works

XGB
XGBoost Classifier
Trained on 10,000+ international matches from 2010 to 2024, with temporal cross-validation to prevent data leakage.
FIFA
FIFA Ranking
Historical FIFA ranking points used as-of-match date — never future data. Captures true strength at time of play.
FORM
Recent Form
Weighted average of last 10 matches: goals scored, conceded, and result — weighted by tournament importance.
H2H
Head-to-Head
Historical win percentage in direct matchups, with exponential time-decay to emphasize recent encounters.
MC
Monte Carlo
100,000 tournament simulations run per update. Every match, every bracket path, every outcome considered.
CAL
Calibration
Probabilities calibrated via Platt scaling. A 30% prediction wins roughly 30% of the time — no inflation.
